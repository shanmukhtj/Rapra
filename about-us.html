About Us

Who We Are

Rapra Constructions is among the finest real estate conglomerates in the city of Hyderabad. Today, 25 years later, ‘Rapra’ is a strong brand associated with unwavering commitment to quality and with customers’ indelible trust thanks to its solid foundations.
